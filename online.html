Online Counselling

Welcome to my Practice!

Working with a counselor online will never replace traditional, face-to-face therapy relationships. Online therapy is another way of caring, one that can reach people who are not getting any other help.

Online counseling is a viable alternative source of help when traditional counseling is not accessible. Many people cannot, or will not, go to a therapist's office. For them, counseling or psychotherapy is not accessible.

"I've discovered that meeting online is just as helpful for clients as meeting in my office."

If traditional counseling is not accessible to you, because distance, cost or other practical factors make a meeting in-person impossible, then book your session online. Meeting online is just as helpful for clients as meeting in the office.
