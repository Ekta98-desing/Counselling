Personal Counselling & Therapy

Welcome to my Practice!

Counseling and Therapy is an exploring process where we see through your heart and mind; where we re-visit some important moments of your life and we let a whole variety of feelings to be experienced. You are not alone anymore - you can safely share all the sufferings and joys of your personal life and together we will work on how to deal with the specific issues that brought you in for counseling.

Our first and foremost priority is your own well-being and we examine various aspects of your relationships of your life -including the relationship with yourself- in order to repair what needs to be changed and empower what needs to be maintained.
